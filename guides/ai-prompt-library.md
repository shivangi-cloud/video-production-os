# AI Prompt Library for Premium Liquor / Product Films

## The three laws
1. **AI never makes text, numbers or logos.** It misspells digits and invents fake labels. AI makes backgrounds only, words are added by hand on top.
2. **Motion words stay small** (slow, subtle, soft, tiny) in image-to-video, big motion melts product labels.
3. **Hard attempt caps.** Write the prompt first, generate once, evaluate honestly, change one word, one more, stop. Log attempts.

## Hero pour (image)
> Extreme macro film still of whisky being poured into a crystal glass in total darkness, golden amber liquid glowing from within, light refracting through the glass, pure black background, cinematic lighting, shallow depth of field, 85mm macro lens, moody premium liquor commercial, photorealistic, dark and elegant, 16:9. No text, no letters, no numbers, no logos, no labels, no hands, no people.

## Dark map (image)
> Minimal map of [REGION] on a near black background, very subtle raised topography texture, thin faint amber lines, the [TERRITORY] region softly glowing, premium dark cartography style, photorealistic texture, 16:9. No labels, no text, no pins, no icons.

## Real store photo, image to video
Motion prompt:
> Slow cinematic camera push in, subtle parallax depth, soft light sweep glinting across the bottles, tiny dust particles floating in the air, everything else perfectly still, photorealistic, warm amber grade

Negative prompt:
> warping, morphing, melting bottles, distorted labels, changing text, people appearing, fast motion, camera shake, zoom out, cartoon, oversaturated

Check every result frame by frame: a warped label is an instant delete.

## Night takeoff (accelerate metaphor)
> Cinematic night scene. A dark airplane begins ON the runway at the left of frame, wheels on the ground, rolling and accelerating for takeoff. It lifts off and climbs into the deep midnight sky, glides for a brief moment as if floating, then suddenly accelerates and shoots away toward the top right corner leaving amber light trails and motion blur. The plane only ever rises, it never descends. Photorealistic, dark and elegant, 16:9.

Negative: landing, descending, approaching from the sky, touching down, daylight, text, logos, people, deformed aircraft

## Night car fly-by (easiest acceleration shot)
> Static camera, cinematic night scene. An empty dark wet street, deep midnight darkness with warm amber street light reflections, still and quiet. Then a matte black unbranded race car with no markings suddenly blasts through the frame from left to right at extreme speed, a blur of amber and gold light trails, gone in an instant. Photorealistic, moody premium commercial film, 16:9.

Negative: sponsor logos, text on car, liveries, numbers, daylight, crowd, people, second car, deformed wheels, camera movement

The words "matte black unbranded, no markings" are what stop AI painting gibberish sponsors on the car.
